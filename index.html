ABOUT

As of writing this, I’m 19, born and raised in Poland. Fluent in Polish and English. I’ve always been interested in art because I see it as a vessel for expressing ideas - as cliché as it sounds. Anything you create begins with an idea and with your taste. I think these are the most valuable things, because unlike technical skills - they cannot be taught. I’ve tried out many different things, as I like to experiment, but I’ve stuck with design and music production. The main purpose of this website is to show my portfolio, but I will guide you to follow my music ventures if you wish to do so. Anyway, let’s work!
