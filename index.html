2 About

I am a graphic designer, musician and music producer, based in Poland.

Yes, I can speak Polish, if you are wondering.

I’m interested in art as a form of expression.. As cliche as it sounds. It impresses me when every element in a composition has purpose, when something is unique, when someone combines two different things that have nothing in common and makes it all work. I value taste and artistry more than technical skills, because they cannot be taught.

I’ve tried out many different things, but music and design are what stuck with me. I still like to experiment, so if you’re interested in other things, maybe there is a way I could help you.

Open to work on: design for brands and artists, campaign visuals, advertisements
